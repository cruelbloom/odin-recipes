Dumplings

Natto's dumpling recipe is world renown for it's delicious and succulent flavors. These protein packed dumplings are extremely easy to make. Please see the ingredient list below:

  • Dumpling shells
  • Minced pork and vegetables
  • Oil
  • Frying pan
  • Fork
  • Appetite
  • Soy sauce

Awesome! It's now time to create these savory dumplings. Here are the steps:

  1. Grab the shells and minced food items
  2. Stack middle of the shells with the minced stuff
  3. Close the chells and seal edges with fork prongs
  4. Light up the stove and spread some oil on the frying pan
  5. Spread dumplings in a circle around the edge of the pan
  6. Wait to hear some sizzling and pour some water into the pan
  7. Close off air with pan lid to steam
  8. Wait a couple of minutes, turn off stove, retrieve dumplings to dish and pour soy sauce
  9. BOOM! Finished!
