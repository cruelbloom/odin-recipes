Fried Chicken

Natto's fried chicken recipe originated in the deep south. Natto was backpacking through the southern states and a gentleman named the "Colonel" handed it to him. Chicken is life and fried chicken is even better life. Give this delicious recipe a shot. Please see the ingredient list below:

  • Whole chicken
  • Panco crumbs
  • Colonel's spices
  • Frying pan
  • Good oil
  • Optional: Hot Sauce

Awesome! It's now time to create some fried chicken. Here are the steps:

  1. Grab the chicken, make sure it's dead, pluck the feathers and cut that bad boy up
  2. Throw chicken limbs into bowl and throw in spices and panko crumbs
  3. Mix the chicken up real good with the ingredients
  4. Light up the stove and apply a generous portion of oil
  5. Throw those limbs inside the pan and fry until golden
  6. Snatch the pieces of chicken onto a plate and apply hot sauce
  7. BOOM! Finished!
