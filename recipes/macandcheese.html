Macaroni and Cheese

Natto's macaroni and cheese is the bees knees. This stuff will ruin all other food for you. Cheesy cheese, crunchy bacon, marinated steak, what else can you want in a dish? Please see the ingredient list below:

  • Elbow macaroni
  • Steak
  • High quality cheese
  • Bacon
  • Butter
  • Milk

Awesome! It's now time to create this sensational macaroni and cheese! Here are the steps:

  1. Grab bacon and cook until crisp
  2. Grab steak and cook until rare
  3. Melt high-quality cheese with stick of butter and milk. After melted together, mix everything
  4. Throw macaroni in pot and boil until soft
  5. Once soft, mix macaroni and melted ingredients together
  6. Throw meat and mix with macaroni and melted ingredients
  7. Pre-heat the oven to 400 degrees and pour ingredients inside dish
  8. Put dish in the oven and wait 20 minutes
  9. BOOM! Finished!
